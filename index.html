    Student Academic Success
    Need academic assistance? we're here with coaching, tutoring, study-skill workshops, and more to help you stay on track. Explore opportunites for financial assistance, community, staying healthy,and being safe.

    what are you looking for?

    Consider Student Academic Success services if you want to:
    • Raise your GPA
    • Ease transition from high school to college
    • Connect with an identity-based group
    • Improve study skills
    • Balance time commitments
    • Stop procrastining and find motivation
    • Get back on track with your classwork
